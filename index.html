Storyboard

Kris is working on a CS assignment late night at the CIT. It is starting to get late and he is getting tired.

He decides to take a walk around in an attempt to wake up a bit. As he walks, he passes the vending machine and sees that they still have Five Hour Energy.

He swipes his ID. While it processes, Kris checks for the number corresponding to the Five Hour Energy.

He types in 25, the code for Five Hour Energy and patiently waits as the machine processes his request.

Kris sees the release mechanism begin and sees his Five Hour Energy drop, ready to be retreived.

Excited to take a sip, Kris pushes the snack door and grabs his Five Hour Energy.
